How to Play:

Pick up to 6 mana types. Up to 2 can be counter mana to reduce the enemy spell's effectiveness by "poisoning" their spell with opposite mana. Be careful, because you could strengthen their spell!

Mana colors are blended to determine the resulting spell. The more of one particular mana type you use, the more potent that mana effect will be. The final color of the spell determines what effects it produces.

Opposite mana types will cancel each other out, so use mana that a similar in "color".

Mana effects

  • Fire: Moderate Fire Damage and burning over time (reduces by half each round)
  • Water: Weak Water Damage and Slow (one less mana picked next round)
  • Earth: Heavy Earth Damage
  • Air: Moderate Air Damage and Dazed (one random mana unusable next round)
  • Stability: Small unaspected damage and chance of ignoring enemy counter mana
  • Volatility: Small unaspected damage and chance of Critical attack (double damage)
  • Physical: Small unaspected damage and chance of Petrification (No action on next round)
  • Mental: Small unaspected damage and chance of Confusion (all mana types are shuffled)
